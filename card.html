Cats

Cats are very popular as pets. The first known association with humans occurred about 9,500 years ago.

  • Scientific Name: Felis silvestris catus
  • Communication: The meow
  • Weight: Usually between 2.5 and 7 kg
  • Life expectancy: 15 to 20 years

Cats are very hygienic animals, and spend many hours a day taking care of cleaning their hair. Cats are also very affectionate and playful, so they are a great companion for adults and children.
